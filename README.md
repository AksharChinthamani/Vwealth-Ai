VWealth AI — Your Personal AI Financial Advisor

VWealth AI is a React + TypeScript web app for tracking and managing NSE/BSE investment portfolios, powered by AI-driven analysis and a voice assistant interface.
It goes beyond a basic portfolio tracker by combining real-time market data, sentiment analysis, and conversational AI to help retail investors and startup founders make sense of their holdings.
Key Features

Portfolio Management — Add and track stocks, ETFs, and mutual funds across 50+ NSE/BSE listed companies, with summaries of total invested amount, diversification, and risk.
AI-Powered Analysis — Get BUY / HOLD / WITHDRAW / REDUCE recommendations for individual assets or your full portfolio, including sector concentration and risk assessment.
Voice Assistant — Ask natural-language questions via speech recognition and get spoken responses (Web Speech API), with dynamically generated charts (bar, pie, line) based on your queries.
Market News & Sentiment — AI-analyzed sentiment scoring on recent news for your top holdings, with headlines and impact summaries.
Financial Goals — Set savings/investment goals with deadlines and progress tracking, plus AI-refreshed insights on whether you're on pace.
Tax Optimization Panel — Estimate tax liability and get AI-generated tax-saving suggestions tailored to Indian tax rules.
Smart Alerts — A notification feed for important portfolio and market events.
User Accounts — Simple email/password auth with role selection (Retail Investor / Startup Founder / Both) and locally persisted, per-user portfolio data.

Tech Stack

Frontend: React 18 + TypeScript, Vite
Charts: Recharts
AI: Google Gemini API (primary), Anthropic Claude API integration
Voice: Web Speech API (speech recognition + text-to-speech)
